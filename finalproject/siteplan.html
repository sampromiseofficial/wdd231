Site Name

Site Purpose

"The purpose of this website is to showcase the portfolio of web development projects completed by Oni Samson Promise, a freelance web developer. The site aims to provide potential clients and collaborators with an overview of Oni's skills, experience, and the types of projects he has worked on. It serves as a professional portfolio to attract new business opportunities and demonstrate his capabilities in web development."

"The site serves as a corporate platform that provides detailed information about Psalm Global Investment’s sectors, including automotive, technology, oil & gas, beverages, and real estate. It showcases the company’s mission, values, and projects while offering visitors opportunities to learn more about the organization, explore business ventures, and submit partnership or investment inquiries through the contact and subscription forms. In essence, the website aims to: Inform visitors about the company’s vision, mission, and leadership. Highlight the products, services, and subsidiaries under the PGI brand. Promote business partnerships and investment opportunities. Provide contact channels for clients and investors. Keep visitors updated through a newsletter and company news."

Scenarios

  • How easily can an international investor or corporate executive find detailed information about Psalm Global Investment’s business sectors and submit a partnership or investment inquiry?
  • Purpose: To evaluate whether the website clearly communicates PGI’s services and provides an intuitive way for potential partners to contact the company or express interest.
  • Can a job seeker or professional quickly learn about Psalm Global Investment’s mission, values, and leadership team to understand whether it’s an organization they would like to work with?
  • Purpose: To determine if the website effectively communicates PGI’s identity, values, and corporate culture to attract skilled professionals or collaborators.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: deep indigo (#27187e) — used for headers, buttons, and navigation background.
  • Text Color 1: soft periwinkle blue (#758bfd) — used for secondary or accent text.
  • Text Color 2: off-white gray (#f1f2f6) — used for body text on dark backgrounds.
  • Background 1 Color: pale lavender blue (#aeb8fe) — used for light backgrounds and highlights.
  • Background 2 Color: bright orange (#ff8600) — used for buttons and interactive elements.

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Roboto", sans-serif — used for all headings and titles.
  • Body Font: "Poppins", Arial, sans-serif — used for paragraphs, buttons, and navigation text.
